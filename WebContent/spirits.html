Wine & Cocktails

Wine

Luciano is proud to present a collection of fine wines from around the world. Wines we offer may compliment any menu selection ~ from a velvety semi-sweet reisling to balance the zesty spice of Pasta Cozze to a big, bold pinot noir to beautifully do battle with the stuffed pork chop or the filet mignon. Or … just to enjoy a final glass of grapes in a lovely atmosphere.

*If you would like to bring your own special bottle we offer a corkage fee of $15.

Please enjoy perusing our Vino Rosso and Vino Bianco wine lists.

Cocktails

The full service bar at Luciano has a wide selection of premium spirits and is ready to create the perfect cocktail for every occasion. Choose a cocktail from our List, or have our bar concoct your favorite libation.

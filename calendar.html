Live Music

Luciano offers elegant and tasteful live music for our patrons to enjoy while they dine. Our musicians perform jazz standards and pop favorites, giving you the sense that you've stepped back in time to find yourself in a 1960s supper club. There's never a cover charge for music at Luciano, but we highly recommend reservations, especially for a table close to the piano.
